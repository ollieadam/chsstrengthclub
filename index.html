Your Neighbor in Wando Woods

Your Neighbor,
Your Strength Coach.

Charleston Strength Club is right here in Wando Woods — and proud to partner with the North Charleston Senior Centers, bringing affordable coach-led strength classes to seniors across the community. Coach Adam Oliver — CSCS certified, former SC Stingrays strength coach, trained at Mike Boyle Strength & Conditioning in Boston — brings pro-level programming right to Wando Woods residents.

We train student athletes, adults, and seniors at prices that work for real people. Whether you want to get faster and jump higher, have flexibility or mobility concerns, were just released from rehab, or simply need programming and accountability for your workouts — Adam's got you covered.

No fancy facility required. We take it to the park or your backyard. Pro-level coaching, real results — and with your coach living right here in Wando Woods, we keep it affordable.

Small groups, flexible scheduling, and a coach who is part of this community.

The Coach

Adam Oliver,
The Real Deal.

Adam didn't get his credentials from a weekend seminar. He earned a Bachelor's in Physical Education & Exercise Science from Norwich University, got his CSCS in 2018, and went straight to work at one of the most respected facilities in the country.

Three years at Mike Boyle Strength & Conditioning in Woburn, MA — the same place that shapes elite-level coaches. Then he brought that knowledge to Charleston and was named S&C Coach for the SC Stingrays hockey team.

Today he coaches adults, seniors, and student athletes across North Charleston with the same disciplined, evidence-based approach — adapted for where you are right now.
