Your Neighbor in Wando Woods

Your Neighbor,
Your Strength Coach.

Charleston Strength Club is right here in Wando Woods — and proud to partner with the North Charleston Senior Centers, bringing affordable coach-led strength classes to seniors across the community. Coach Adam Oliver — CSCS certified, former SC Stingrays strength coach, trained at Mike Boyle Strength & Conditioning in Boston — brings pro-level programming right to North Charleston.

We train student athletes, adults, and seniors at prices that work for real people. Whether you want to get faster and jump higher, have mobility concerns, were just released from rehab, or simply need programming and accountability — Adam's got you covered.

No fancy facility required. We take it to the park or your backyard. Pro-level coaching, real results — with a coach who's part of this community.
