The Class

What toExpect.

This isn't a spin class or a bootcamp. It's real strength and conditioning — programmed by a CSCS coach who spent years training professional hockey players. Every session is different, every session has a purpose.

Save Your Spot

Who It's For

Built for Everyone.

I
Adults

Whether you've never lifted before or you've been training for years, the class is coached to your level. Modifications always available.

II
Athletes

In-season or off-season, group training builds strength and conditioning that carries over directly to your sport.

III
Beginners

Never stepped in a gym? Good. Adam will teach you to move correctly from day one. No experience required, no judgment, ever.
